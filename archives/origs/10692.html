Sea's Forrest Li Credits Singapore In Businessman of The Year Win

冬海集团首席执行官李小冬成为新加坡年度最佳商人


While there is a stereotype that business leaders come to Singapore only after they have made it big, Forrest Li, head honcho of Internet giant Sea, emphasises that this is not his story.


尽管人们总是有一种刻板印象，认为商界领袖只有功成身就后才会来新加坡，但互联网巨头、大型游戏企业冬海集团的创始人、主席兼集团首席执行官李小冬强调，他与人们印象中的商人不一样。


The entrepreneur was recently named Businessman of the Year at the 35th Singapore Business Awards (SBA) and shares his warming story during his acceptance speech.


这位企业家最近被提名为第35届新加坡商业大奖的年度最佳商人，并在他的获奖感言中分享了他温暖人心的故事。


Forrest Li at the at the 35th Singapore Business Awards (SBA)

李小冬出席第35届新加坡商业大奖颁奖典礼

Photo Credit 图片来源: Business Times


“Singapore took me in when I was a nobody. I came here with barely any savings, and a hundred thousand dollars in student loan debt. My wife and I could only rent one bedroom in a three-room HDB flat in Braddell,” he shares. "Despite this, the Singaporean community accepted me, for which I will always be grateful. Long before I had any success, they made me feel welcome."


“当我还是一无所有时，新加坡接纳了我。我来这里时几乎没有任何积蓄，还欠了10万美元的学生贷款。我和妻子只能租住在布莱德的一套三居室组屋中的一间卧室。尽管如此，新加坡还是接受了我，对此我将永远心存感激。早在我有所成就之前，他们就让我感到被欢迎。”


"Business is a team sport, and I am lucky to have over 3,000 excellent teammates here in Singapore, and thousands more around the world. Tonight, this award belongs not to me but to all of us," Li said. He credits Singapore's Economic Development Board with introducing his firm to tech giant Tencent, a backer of Sea.


“商业是一项团队运动，我很幸运在新加坡有3,000多名优秀的队友，以及遍布在世界各地的数千名队友。今晚，这个奖不属于我，而是属于我们团队的所有人。”他感谢新加坡经济发展局将他的公司介绍给科技巨头腾讯，而腾讯是冬海集团的投资方。

How Singapore’s Sea Is Surfing Southeast Asia’s Digital Wave

新加坡的“冬海”如何顺应东南亚数字化浪潮


When Apple founder Steve Jobs gave a famous speech to a class of Stanford students in 2005 – exhorting them to “stay hungry, stay foolish” – one of those he inspired was Forrest Li.


2005年，苹果公司创始人史蒂夫·乔布斯在斯坦福大学为学生们作了一场精彩的演讲，劝勉他们要“求知若饥，虚心若愚”。李小冬就是被他激励的人之一。


Fifteen years on, Forrest’s startup is the most valuable listed company in Southeast Asia. Singapore-headquartered Sea, which operates online gaming, e-commerce, and digital financial services, is among the biggest beneficiaries of the COVID-19 pandemic, which has given a boost to online services across the world .


15年过去了，李小冬的公司成为东南亚最有价值的上市公司。总部位于新加坡的冬海集团主营业务是在线游戏、电子商务和数字金融服务。由于新冠疫情的影响推动了全球在线服务的发展，冬海集团也成为新冠疫情的最大受益者之一。


Forrest says that the region’s post-pandemic economy could become an opportunity for Southeast Asia.

李小冬表示，新冠疫情后的经济形势可能成为东南亚的一个机遇。

Photo Credit 图片来源: KrAsia


Forrest, now Sea’s CEO, said the digitalisation driven by the pandemic is “positive” for the Southeast Asian economy. He said his company will continue to aggressively capture untapped opportunities in the region and beyond. “We don’t want to compromise our growth potential,” he said.


冬海集团的首席执行官李小冬表示，新冠疫情推动的数字化对东南亚经济来说是“积极的”。他的公司将继续积极抓住当地和其他地区尚未开发的机会。“我们不想破坏我们的增长潜力，”他说道。
